About me

Some stories of my life

Hi!

I’m a Brazilian full-stack developer with a background in Environmental Engineering. I completed my Bachelor’s degree in Brazil and later moved to the United States for a Master’s in Environmental Engineering (MSc), specializing in Solid Waste Management.

After relocating to Germany, I realized my previous field offered limited opportunities—and that’s what led me to tech. I fell in love with the creativity and problem-solving involved in building software, especially the challenge of making many different technologies work seamlessly together.

I’m highly organized, detail-oriented, and adaptable, and I enjoy working across the entire stack—from database design to building intuitive user interfaces. Right now, I’m looking for an internship or a junior position as a software developer (full-stack or web), where I can continue learning and contribute to real, meaningful projects.

Outside of coding, you
can usually find me
hiking with my dog.
